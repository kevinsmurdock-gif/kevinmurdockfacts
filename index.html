The Federal Record

CDC MMWR · Vol. 70 · No. 49 · December 10, 2021

Federal Public Health Document
The CDC's Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report documents the CDC's use of pooled COVID-19 testing through the Increasing Community Access to Testing (ICATT) program, executed via the eTrueNorth contractor — the same contractor pathway used by Premier Medical Laboratory Services.
The published federal record establishes that the pooled-testing methodology deployed in Greenville was the same methodology the CDC chose to deploy nationally through its own contractor pathway.
Source: CDC MMWR, Vol. 70, No. 49, December 10, 2021. Published and publicly available at cdc.gov.
